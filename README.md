## 🚀 What I'm Working On

### 🔬 InsightScribe
> *AI-powered qualitative research intelligence for product teams & UX researchers*

A B2B SaaS platform that turns interview recordings into structured, searchable insights. I own the entire frontend — drag-and-drop uploads, chat UI, citation timestamps, processing status badges, the works. We're targeting Series A/B product teams who are drowning in interview data and need answers fast.

---

### 📓 AI/ML Teaching Materials
> *Helping engineers understand agentic AI from the ground up*

I write detailed instructional scripts for Jupyter notebooks — real code, real systems, real explanations. Topics include:

- 🏦 **Fidelity Agentic RAG** — multi-step retrieval with tool-calling agents
- 🖥️ **Dell Products Agent** — structured product recommendation pipelines
- 🏥 **Northwestern Memorial Hospital** — call center AI with routing logic
- 🛡️ **Allstate Claims Processing** — agentic document analysis workflows
